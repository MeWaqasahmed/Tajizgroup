Organic Farming

At Tazij Meats, we believe in working with nature, not against it. Our entire supply chain consists of 100% grass-fed animals raised on natural pastures by over 35 certified farmers and dealers across Pakistan. These animals graze freely on grass, shrubs, and herbs without any growth hormones, artificial feed, or confined housing. The result is meat that's naturally lower in fat and cholesterol, higher in Omega-3 fatty acids and CLA, and carries the authentic flavor of Pakistan's rich agricultural landscape — delivering superior nutrition and taste to health-conscious consumers worldwide.

What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is a farming system that works with nature's own forces. Organically farmed animals are not only good for your health but also great for the environment.

Our cattle, lamb and goats are bred and raised in natural environments and graze on grass, shrubs, herbs, plants and weed. Meat produced from a grass-fed animal is significantly lower in fat and cholesterol than grain-fed animals. Grass-fed meat is higher in Omega-3 fatty acids and CLA (Conjugated Linoleic Acid) — an anti-carcinogenic associated with significant health benefits.

Grass-fed meat also carries a flavour unique to the pasture and environment in which it was raised. We are seeing more discerning consumers moving away from bland grain-fed meat towards tasty, natural, grass-fed Pakistani meat — particularly across the Middle East. Tazij Meats aims to provide exceptionally high quality meat products produced with the consumer's health and the environment in mind.
